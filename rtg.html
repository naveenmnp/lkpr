ABOUT RTG

  • THE RTG SILK is one of the textile showrooms of the Regent group dedicated only to silk sarees and it is established on 27 September 2020. There are three textile showroom, two in the name of Regent one in kovilpatti, and another in Aruppukottai and the third one is THE RTG SILK

  • It is a family shop with dress collections for all, including newborn babies are available. For gents all readymade dress from shirting, suiting, trousers, dhoti, inners, etc., For women saree, chudidhar, lehenga, Midi, frock, tops, and all types of western clothes

  • There are three floors in the RTG silk and 150 people are working there simultaneously. Moreover, an entire floor is dedicated exclusively to silk sarees only. Two car parking spaces are available one with 2000 sq. ft and another with 5000 sq. ft, apart from this, free auto service from the Bus stand is also available
